=RSS4Twitter

RSS4Twitter can parse your rss feed and throw the message to your twitter account.  
Tested on MAC,linux(ubuntu).

==Requirement:
*twitter4r
*tinyurl

==HOW TO:

1.setup the account information in the twitter.yml
2.setup the rss feeds information in the twitter.yml 
3.run the ./rss4twitter(chmod +x rss4twitter)
4.and then you can see the result in your twitter !!!
